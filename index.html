Program Overview

Primary Goals

  • Build lean muscle volume
  • Develop 6-pack abs
  • Strengthen weak areas (back, shoulders, arms, traps)
  • Maintain athletic performance
  • Achieve V-taper physique

Training Style

  • Athletic + Heavy Weight Hybrid
  • Olympic lifts & Explosive movements
  • Progressive overload focus
  • High-frequency upper body training
  • Functional strength emphasis

Expected Results

  • 7-10kg fat loss (85kg → 75-78kg)
  • 2-4kg lean muscle gain
  • 4-6cm shoulder width increase
  • 2-4cm arm size increase
  • Visible 6-pack abs
